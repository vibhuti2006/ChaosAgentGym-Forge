## Key observations

- **Base model: 0% success.** Action mix shows 48% RETRY and 0% VERIFY — the untrained model never issues a VERIFY, just spins on RETRYs and hits the 8-step budget every episode. **Worse than random** (which at least sometimes rolls into a VERIFY).
- **Trained model: 89% success** — matches the hand-coded scripted oracle exactly, on the same held-out seeds.
- **Action distribution converges** to the canonical recipe (40% GET, 40% PUT, 20% VERIFY) — i.e. the agent has internalised the PUT → GET → PUT → GET → VERIFY pattern.
- The 11% failure rate is the chaos working as designed (some chaos sequences are unrecoverable in 8 steps).
